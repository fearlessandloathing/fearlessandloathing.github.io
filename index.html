Dear readers,

I founded Fearless and Loathing in 2009 to be an independent source of news for Oberlin College and as such established F+L as an LLC.

Last year's editorial staff opted to charter as a student organization, intending to better serve the Oberlin student body.

We've planned to handle this split like a project fork which we are in the process of working out. Soon they can be starting off with a copy of all of F+L's content, along with the followers they've gained on Facebook and Twitter. I wish them well on their endeavor. Find them at http://disdainfulyouth.com, on Facebook at http://facebook.com/disdainfulyouth, and on Twitter @disdainfulyouth.

Make no mistake, fearlessandloathing.com L.L.C. remains active and independent from Oberlin College.

Love,
David Edward Clark '12, Founder and Owner
