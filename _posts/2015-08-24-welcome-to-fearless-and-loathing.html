Dear readers,

I founded Fearless and Loathing in 2009 to be an independent source of news for Oberlin College and as such established F+L as an LLC.

Last year’s editorial staff decided to charter as a student organization, intending to better serve the Oberlin student body.

We’ve handled this split like a project fork. I’ve given the former editors of F+L a complete copy of F+L’s filesystem and database, and I wish them well on their endeavor, found at disdainfulyouth.com, on Facebook at facebook.com/disdainfulyouth, and on Twitter @disdainfulyouth.

Make no mistake, fearlessandloathing.com L.L.C. remains active and independent from Oberlin College. Check the new About section for more on the new direction.

Love,
David Edward Clark ‘12, Founder and Owner
